    About Us

    Kenya Beacon News is an independent media platform delivering accurate, timely, and credible news across Kenya and beyond. Guided by integrity and professionalism, we aim to inform, educate, and empower citizens. As "The Light of Reliable News," we shine clarity on national issues, amplify diverse voices, and uphold truth in every story.

    Mission: To provide factual, balanced, and impactful journalism that informs the public and strengthens democracy in Kenya.

    Vision: To become one of Kenya's most trusted and influential news platforms, recognized for credibility, innovation, and excellence in reporting.
